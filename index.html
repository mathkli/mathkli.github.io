Biography

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science (AMSS), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS). I attained the Phd degree at the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science (AMSS), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS) and University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), under the supervision of Prof. Xiaohong Jia. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree of information and computing science from Dalian University of Technology in 2019.

My research interest includes computer aided geometric design, computer aided design and computer graphics.
